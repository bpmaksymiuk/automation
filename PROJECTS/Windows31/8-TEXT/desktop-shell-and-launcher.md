# Desktop Shell and Launcher Copy Contract

## DesktopSurface

| Field key | Character limit | Final copy | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| `desktop_window_title` | 24 | `Program Manager` | Uses the period-correct shell title. |
| `desktop_group_accessories_title` | 18 | `Accessories` | Familiar group name for utility apps. |
| `desktop_group_games_title` | 18 | `Games` | Compact group label for Minesweeper. |
| `desktop_status_hint` | 56 | `Double-click to open. Tap once on touch screens.` | Explains both desktop and mobile activation without extra chrome. |

## LauncherIcon

| Field key | Character limit | Final copy | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| `launcher_notepad_label` | 16 | `Notepad` | Short, iconic launcher label. |
| `launcher_paint_label` | 16 | `Paint` | Short launcher wording for fast scanning. |

## Tone Application

- Treat the shell as a room with familiar labels, not as a modern app hub.
- Keep launcher wording simple enough to sit beside period-style icons.
