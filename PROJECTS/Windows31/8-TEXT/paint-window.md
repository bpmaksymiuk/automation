| Field key | Character limit | Final copy | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|
| `paint_window_title` | 24 | `Paintbrush` | Historically legible window title with a period feel. |
| `paint_tool_pencil_label` | 12 | `Pencil` | Primary drawing tool label. |
| `paint_tool_eraser_label` | 12 | `Eraser` | Alternate tool label. |
| `paint_tool_clear_label` | 12 | `Clear` | Compact label for wiping the canvas. |
| `paint_palette_group_label` | 18 | `Colors` | Short visible heading above the swatches. |

## Tone Application

- Keep tool names concrete and glanceable.
- Use one period nod in the title, then stay modern enough to remain immediately usable.
